## 11 to 19

Formed with 十 (sup^6^) followed by a digit from 1 to 9.
For example, Twelve is 十二 (sup^6^ nyi^4^) aka 10 and 2.

| Numeral | Chinese | Romanisation |
|---------|---------|--------------|
| 11      | 十一    | sup^6^ yit^5^    |
| 12      | 十二    | sup^6^ nyi^4^    |
