## 11 to 19

Formed with 十 (sup<sup>6</sup>) followed by a digit from 1 to 9.
For example, Twelve is 十二 (sup<sup>6</sup> nyi<sup>4</sup>) aka 10 and 2.

|---------|---------|--------------|
